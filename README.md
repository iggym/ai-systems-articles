## 🌐 **Purpose**
This repository is a growing collection of **deep, practical, and system‑level articles** exploring the emerging disciplines that define modern AI engineering:  
- **Context Engineering**  
- **Context Orchestration**  
- **AI Verifiability & Governance**  

Each article is written for engineers, architects, founders, and researchers who want to build **reliable**, **autonomous**, and **production‑grade** AI systems—not just models.

## 📚 **Articles Library**

### **1. Context Orchestration**  
**“The shift from coding to context orchestration.”**  
Published: **2026‑06‑25**  
Path: `articles/context-orchestration.html`  
Context orchestration is becoming the new backbone of AI systems. Instead of writing logic, engineers now design **flows, constraints, and environments** that shape model behavior. This article explores the architectural patterns behind this shift and how to build systems that behave predictably under uncertainty.

### **2. Verifiability & Governance**  
**“Accountability infrastructure is becoming the substrate on which trustworthy AI autonomy is built.”**  
Published: **2026‑05‑18**  
Path: `articles/verifiability.html`  
As AI systems become more agentic and autonomous, **verifiability** becomes non‑optional. This article breaks down the governance primitives—logging, evaluation, constraints, oversight loops—that allow autonomous systems to operate safely and transparently.

---

### **3. Context Engineering**  
**“Context engineering is reducing uncertainty, not adding information.”**  
Published: **2026‑04‑02**  
Path: `articles/context-engineering.html`  
Context engineering is the discipline of shaping the *environment* in which AI systems operate. This article introduces the principles behind uncertainty reduction, context lifecycle design, and how to build systems that maintain coherence across long‑running tasks.
